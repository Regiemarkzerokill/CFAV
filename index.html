About CFAV

The Campus First Aid Volunteer (CFAV) is a student-based organization that promotes first aid awareness, emergency response training, and humanitarian service. Its purpose is to prepare students to respond during accidents, disasters, and medical emergencies inside and outside the school.

Organization Details

CFAV conducts drills, trainings, outreach programs, and emergency support activities. Members are trained in CPR, first aid, disaster preparedness, and teamwork leadership.
